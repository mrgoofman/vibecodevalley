## 9. Your Rights Under GDPR

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

### 9.1 Right of Access (Art. 15 GDPR)
Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

### 9.2 Right to Rectification (Art. 16 GDPR)
Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.

### 9.3 Right to Erasure (Art. 17 GDPR)
Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten"), subject to legal obligations.

### 9.4 Right to Restriction (Art. 18 GDPR)
Request limitation of processing in certain circumstances.

### 9.5 Right to Data Portability (Art. 20 GDPR)
Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

### 9.6 Right to Object (Art. 21 GDPR)
Object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing.

### 9.7 Right to Withdraw Consent (Art. 7(3) GDPR)
Withdraw consent at any time for consent-based processing.

### 9.8 Right to Lodge a Complaint
File a complaint with your local data protection authority.

**To exercise your rights, contact us via the information provided at the end of this policy.**
